1. Introduction

The purpose of this visual toolkit is to create a single reference point for designers and developers.

A unified guide/visual toolkit will create a visual language and vocabulary that both designers and developers can share, allowing for better communication, smoother onboarding and consistency across a project.

It will also make it easier to see where our design breaks, check how components adapt to different screen sizes, and will force us to think of things as a whole rather than a specific page. A boiler plate list of components for every project can also act as a reminder of things that may get missed in the design.
